# CS-330-Comp-Graphic-and-Visualization
Portfolio for CS-330 Comp Graphic and Visualiztion

Throughout this project, I approached engineering software with a structured and iterative mindset. One of the most important skills I developed was the ability to break down a complex problem into manageable components, such as scene construction, lighting, textures, and camera controls. I followed a design process that began with understanding the project requirements, planning the structure of the scene, and incrementally implementing features while testing each change. This approach allowed me to identify issues early and refine the project without introducing unnecessary complexity. The tactics I used—such as modular design, incremental development, and frequent testing—are directly transferable to future software engineering projects, especially those involving complex systems.

When developing programs for this project, I relied heavily on iterative development and debugging strategies. Building the 3D scene required constant refinement, particularly when working with lighting, transformations, and textures. Iteration played a key role, as each milestone built upon the previous one and required revisiting earlier code to improve functionality or visual accuracy. Over time, my approach to coding evolved from focusing solely on getting the program to run to emphasizing clarity, structure, and maintainability. This progression helped lead to the successful completion of the project and strengthened my overall development process.

Computer science has played a critical role in helping me progress toward my long-term goals. Through computational graphics and visualization, I gained practical experience applying mathematical concepts, problem-solving skills, and software design principles in a visual and interactive context. These skills are valuable in my future educational pathway, as they reinforce core programming concepts and strengthen my ability to work with advanced systems. Professionally, the experience gained from this course has prepared me to contribute to projects involving graphics, simulations, or interactive applications, and it has enhanced my ability to think analytically while creating efficient and visually meaningful solutions.
